Igniting Young Chefs: Empowering Kids in the Kitchen!

Welcome to Kids Menu, where cooking becomes an extraordinary adventure for kids! We're passionate about finding and creating recipes that inspire children to actively participate in the culinary world.

Cooking isn't just about nourishing our bodies; it's an opportunity for kids to develop vital life skills, ignite their creativity, and foster a lifelong love for food.

Learn More!

Our dedicated team of culinary enthusiasts has curated a collection of recipes specifically designed to excite and inspire young chefs. From quick snacks to mouthwatering main courses and delightful desserts, our recipes cater to every palate.

But our mission extends beyond sharing recipes. We aim to foster togetherness and create lasting memories in the kitchen. Cooking becomes a platform for building strong family connections, where kids and parents can learn from each other and create moments of joy and laughter.

With easy-to-follow instructions, helpful tips, and engaging visuals, our kid-friendly recipes make it fun and simple for young chefs to get cooking. By empowering kids with culinary skills, we equip them with tools for making healthier choices, fostering creativity, and boosting self-confidence.

Join us on this incredible culinary journey, where the kitchen becomes a playground and meals become masterpieces. Discover the joy of cooking with kids at Kids Menu, inspiring the next generation of chefs, one delicious recipe at a time.

Rainbow Pizza

Ingredients:

  • store-bought pizza base
  • 1 cup tomato sauce (store-bought or homemade)
  • vegetables in different colours, e.g. tomatoes, orange pepper, yellow pepper, broccoli, red onion, and kalamata olives

Berry Banana Shake

Ingredients:

  • 250ml milk of choice
  • 1 cup of berries, e.g. strawberries, blueberries (fresh or frozen)
  • one banana

Kiddie Crunch Mix

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup plain or frosted animal crackers
  • 1 cup bear-shaped crackers
  • 1 cup miniature pretzels
  • 1 cup salted peanuts
  • 1 cup M&M's
  • 1 cup yogurt- or chocolate-covered raisins

Muffin Trays Snack

Ingredients:

  • cheese
  • raspberry
  • carrot
  • blueberry
  • crackers

Owl Yoghurt Bowl

Ingredients:

  • 150g organic natural yogurt
  • 1 tsp honey (optional)
  • ¼ small apple
  • 1 red grape
  • small handful organic cornflakes

Sandwich Kebab

Ingredients:

  • 4 slices bread
  • 1 tbs butter
  • 2 slices leg ham
  • 2 slices cheese
  • 6 baby cornichons
  • 6 red grape tomatoes
